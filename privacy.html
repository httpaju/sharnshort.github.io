We use Google Ads on our website, which involves the use of cookies and other tracking technologies to collect and use information about you. This may include:

6. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website and to enable the functionality of Google Ads. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of certain features on our site and the personalization of ads.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can delete the information.
